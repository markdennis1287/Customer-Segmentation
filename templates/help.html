Upload Guide

Learn how to upload your data for segmentation analysis.

Steps to Upload Your Data:

  1. Prepare Your File: Ensure your data file is in .csv or .xlsx format and contains headers (e.g., "Customer ID", "Age", "Spending Score").
  2. File Size: Keep your file size below 10MB to ensure smooth processing.
  3. Select Your File: On the Upload Page, click "Choose a File" and select your prepared file.
  4. Submit the File: Click the "Upload and Segment" button to begin the upload and segmentation process.
  5. Wait for Results: A loading indicator will appear. Once the process is complete, you will see the segmentation results.

Tips for Preparing Your Data:

  • Ensure your data has no missing or invalid values.
  • Use descriptive column headers, such as "Age", "Income", "Spending Score".
  • If using Excel files (.xlsx), ensure the data is in the first sheet.
